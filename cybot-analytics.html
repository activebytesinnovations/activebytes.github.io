CYBOT™ is your intelligent analytical threat hunting solution

View Datasheet

In the proactive approach method of cybersecurity framework, data aggregation, attribution, and analysis play an important role. Traditional security analytics methods lack the ability to combine massive amounts of raw data into one place& format it for further processing. Hence a major portion of the valuable log goes unattended by the analysts. This is a very risky way of doing your business. The un attended logs may have threat elements that can cause damage to your enterprise, your assets and most importantly your customer data!

Our expert team designed the CYBOT™ Platform with a Big Data Analytic Engine that can handle huge data from an enterprise IT infrastructure, with best-in-class analytics and processing capability. We’ve built hundreds of Dashboards and Alerts out of the box for both compliance and security analytics purposes. Additionally you will have access to our content library that get updated with new Dashboards and Alerts .This smart and intelligent updation continuously improve the hunting efficiency of the platform.

Huge Data extraction from OS, system behavior, communication between systems and to external IP addresses, common user behavior etc. and hence no data is missed out
Analysis of logs of OS binaries execution and registry changes, thereby investigating even the critical areas for threats.
Extraction of data related to file creation, deletion and modification activities, other systems/application logs. This helps to detect an insider or external adversary action & behavior.
